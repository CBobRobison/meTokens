Welcome to meTokns!
===================
DeFi Enabled Personal Tokens
############################

meTokens is a personal token protocol built on the Ethereum blockchain. 
Tokenize yourself, let anyone invest in your future success, and develop an audience of supporters 
who are incentivized to see you succeed in every possible way.

meTokens introduce a novel kind of `automated market maker (AMM) alogorithm 
<https://medium.com/anchordao-lab/automated-market-maker-amm-algorithms-and-its-future-f2d5e6cc624a>`_, 
called an Automated Discount Market Maker (ADMM). ADMMs trustlessly create flash sales for anyone to buy & spend 
another person’s meToken. Even though the spender earns a discount for using the ADMM (e.g. "15% off 1 hour of consultation"), 
the meToken issuer still earn the full value of the sale at no loss (i.e. as if there was no 15% off discount).

meTokens introduce a novel kind of automated market maker (AMM), called an Automated Discount Market Maker (ADMM). 
ADMMs trustlessly create flash sales for anyone to buy & spend another person’s meToken. 
Even though the spender earns a discount for using the ADMM, meToken issuers still earn the full value of the sale at no loss.


meTokens are beneficially unique because of 3 unique features:

1. **Can be issued by anyone** - regardless of if you have already found succes or are just beginning your 
    journey, meToken will work for you. This is a tool for anybody who works in public (developers, designers, 
    NFT artist, writers, content creators, etc); not just influencers.
2. **Are pump-n-dump resistant** - if there is ever a mass selloff or flash crash event in the market, meTokens 
    have a trustless, built-in safety measure to limit any potential market downside that could occur. The truest fans 
    who hold through market volitility are rewarded in trading fees.
